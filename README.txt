B1 Goethe Vocabulary Trainer

This package contains:
- index.html: the app
- 3288 imported vocabulary entries from the Goethe B1 PDF + your custom words

Important:
- iPhone Files may show HTML as a preview. For phone use, upload index.html to Netlify Drop or another static host, then open the link in Chrome.
- Progress is saved in the browser that opens the app.
- Some imported Goethe entries are first-pass and may show "Add/check meaning"; you can edit meanings inside the app.
